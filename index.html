Diphthongs “ai” and “ay”


The "ai" team:

  • Sounds like the letter A
  • Hides at the start or middle of words
  • Never comes at the end
  • Examples:Rain,Wait,Paint

The "ay" team:

  • Also sounds like the letter A
  • Loves to be at the end of words
  • Examples:Play,Day,Way
  • Words like “Crayon” and “Always” are special case where the diphthong “ay” comes in the middle

Remember, when you're reading:

  • If you see "ai" in the middle, say "A"!
  • If you see "ay" at the end, say "A"!
